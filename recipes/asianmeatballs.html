Spicy Asain Style Meatballs

I have not actually made this yet but I am keen to try it

Ingredients

For the meatballs

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 3 tsps sesame oil
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 spring onions, thinly sliced

For the sauce

  • 1/4 hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsps minced ginger
  • 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Sesame seeds, sliced green chilli, cofiander leaves and sliced spring onion to serve

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Place mince, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, breadcrumbs, spring onions and eggs in a large mixing bowl. Bring together with hands.
  3. Shape into bite-sized meatballs.
  4. Transfer tothe oven and bake for 10 minutes or until cooked through.
  5. Combine the sauce ingredients and heat up on a small pan for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Dunk meatballs into the glaze so they are completely covered and serve with greens on top
